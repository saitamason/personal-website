Education and Academic Achievements

Throughout my educational journey, I embarked on a diverse range of subjects that have shaped the person I am today. From my early foray into the world of technology to my pursuit of understanding the complexities of human behavior, each step has been a transformative experience.

I have always had a fondness for tinkering with computers. As my fascination with technology grew, so did my passion for web development, leading me to explore this field more deeply over the years. My introduction to the world of programming began at the tender age of eleven when I created my very first HTML document. Soon later, I started writing small websites for my friends and family and even took on more significant projects, such as redesigning my primary school's official website.

In 2012, my innocent hobby took a more serious turn when I enrolled in a leading electronic technical school with a strong emphasis on mathematics and information technology. During this period I acquired a diverse set of technical skills and knowledge, including expertise in algorithms, data structures, databases, servers, networks, Linux, and software development. Along the way, I gained proficiency in various programming languages, while also mastering essential programming paradigms and design patterns.

The year 2016 marked a significant milestone as I graduated with the highest GPA in my class and earned professional qualifications in the development of web applications and database administration. Furthermore, I achieved an outstanding score of 98% in the advanced-level computer science exam.

While pursuing further education in information technology seemed like a natural progression, I made a well-considered decision to take a different path after completing the first semester of applied computer science studies at Bydgoszcz University of Science and Technology. The allure of psychology was undeniable, and I believed it was essential to take a temporary break from the programmer's journey to gain fresh perspectives and open my mind to new horizons. This period of self-discovery allowed me to better understand myself and the people around me, and it has undoubtedly influenced the person I am today.

Transitioning into the field of psychology, I embarked on a five-year journey at Kazimierz Wielki University, culminating in earning a master's degree in psychology in 2022 with the highest GPA. Throughout my academic pursuit, I served as the prefect of the year, demonstrating strong leadership and organizational skills. My scientific achievements were recognized through consistent scholarships, and I actively engaged in various scientific clubs, holding roles such as member, president, and vice president. I even had the privilege of representing the Student Council.

During my time studying psychology, I actively contributed to over fifteen research projects, delving into diverse topics such as psychometrics, motherhood and parenting, alcohol addiction, aggression, work, depression, and even the impact of COVID-19. Additionally, I engaged in studies related to cognitive and neurological psychology, exploring subjects like attention, working memory, and reasoning. Some of these projects received accolades at national and international scientific conferences.

Notably, I actively participated in research projects supported by the Ministry of Science and Higher Education through various grant programs. Working in the EEG laboratory, I conducted individual sessions with participants and processed EEG signals using the MATLAB programming environment. Furthermore, I had the privilege of co-authoring two posters presented at international scientific conferences in Prague and Warsaw.

Beyond my scientific pursuits, I found fulfillment in volunteering for various educational campaigns, where I contributed as a workshop leader, designer, and conductor. My graphic design skills also came to the fore as I created logos and posters for several organizations, including the Faculty of Psychology. Moreover, I explored my artistic side by being part of a choir, performing at different cultural and charity events.

During my fourth year of psychological studies, I decided to delve into another passion of mine: physical education. Simultaneously pursuing this field alongside psychology, I also engaged in research and served as the vice president of one of the student's scientific clubs. Additionally, I acquired professional certifications as a personal trainer and fitness instructor, expanding my knowledge in the realm of physical training. Although I completed the first year of physical education studies with the highest GPA, I ultimately chose to resign from the program to focus on my psychological master's thesis.

In my final year of psychological studies, I was honored to be selected as a grant laureate, sponsored by Iceland, Liechtenstein, and Norway. This exceptional opportunity allowed me to participate in a transformative student exchange program at Inland Norway University of Applied Sciences. The experience not only enriched my academic journey but also deepened my understanding of Scandinavian culture and philosophy, while significantly improving my English fluency.

My academic and research accomplishments culminated in the prestigious Award of the Rector of Kazimierz Wielki University, recognizing me as the Best Graduate and Student of the academic year 2021/2022. Additionally, my dedication and hard work were acknowledged with an award for producing the best master's thesis in the field of psychology. Based on this work, I wrote a scientific article that was published in the esteemed Lublin Pedagogical Yearbook.

After all these years of diverse academic pursuits and self-discovery, I have come full circle and returned to my first love: programming and web development. Today, I work as a front-end developer, combining my passion for technology with the invaluable insights gained from my educational journey. The fusion of my critical thinking skills from psychology and my technical expertise enables me to approach software development with a unique and holistic perspective, crafting intuitive and user-centric digital experiences. I am excited to continue growing in this ever-evolving field and to embark on new challenges that lie ahead.
